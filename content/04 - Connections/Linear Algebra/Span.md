**Idea**: For a set of vectors to **span** a space, it simply means that any vector in the space can be expressed as a [[Linear Combination]] of the vectors in that set.


**Span**: Let $M = \{\vec{v_1},\vec{v_2},.....,\vec{v_m}\}$ be a finite collection of vectors in the vector space $S$. The span of $M$, denoted by $span(M)$ or $span\{\vec{v_1},\vec{v_2},.....,\vec{v_m}\}$ is the set of all [[Linear Combination]] of vectors in $M$:
That is:
$$
	span(M) = \{ \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i \vec{v_i} : a_i \in R \}
$$
