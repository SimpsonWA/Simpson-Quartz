
Iteration of Line search methods:
$$
x_{k+1} = x_k + \alpha_k p_k, \quad k = 0, 1, 2, \ldots 
$$
for some vector $p_k \in \mathbb{R}^n$ and step sizes $\alpha_k \geq 0$.

The success of the line search method is dependent on effective choices of both the direction $p_k$ and the step length $\alpha_k$
